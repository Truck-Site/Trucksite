About

Our Story

We don’t just sell equipment; we deliver the tools that enable you to run your business. Providing reliable equipment that you can count on translates into maximized return on your investment. What enables us to do this is our dedicated team of professionals. We invest in our employees, and they in turn exceed the expectations of our customers.

Our company was founded by Michael Leschinski who currently leads our team and oversees the day-to-day operations. Born with an entrepreneurial spirit and an intrinsic ability to form long-term business relationships, Michael’s path to ownership of the Truck Site business started from an early age. As the 4th child in a family of twelve with limited resources, Michael quickly became a hard worker. When his family emigrated from the former USSR as refugees, they were required to temporarily reside in Austria and Italy before arriving to the United States. Even at the young age of 12, Michael found industrious ways to earn money, like washing windshields on the intersections near his transitory home in Italy.

Once he and his family arrived to the U.S., he continued to work hard by organizing car washes, re-selling merchandise, and making trade deals. It was shortly after high school that Michael began his career in the industry by working for Mission City Leasing, a reputable vehicle dealer, in Santa Barbara, CA. This is where Michael became passionate about this industry and fell in love with selling to other businesses. Over the next 9 years he learned all of the intricacies of locating, buying, and selling trucks to businesses. While working and shadowing the owner John Mullins, Michael learned many things but especially the conviction that honesty defines character and today that conviction remains true with every unit that leaves our facility.

At Truck Site our specialty is CaliforniaClean®, ex-government owned trucks. We encourage you to take a look at what our customers are saying, browse our online inventory, and then give us a call. We would love to hear from you.

Over a decade
in business

Truck Site’s history began in 2004 in a small 350 square foot office with two parking spots that we shared with another business. For the past ten years we have enjoyed steady growth and today we employ over 60 dedicated long-term employees, operate in a modern full service facility, and have an inventory comprised of over four hundred vehicles to choose from.

Used Commercial Trucks

At Truck Site, we don’t have a problem with depreciation, as long as someone else is paying it! As experts in the used commercial truck market, we know that our customers want a great deal on quality trucks and equipment that come from ex-municipal and government agencies. We rarely carry privately owned used vehicles, to ensure that you’re getting trucks that have been well-serviced and properly maintained during their lifetime.

What does this mean for your business? It means you’re getting a quality used commercial vehicle that has seen regular service and maintenance, and is ready for many more years of work. Our trucks are inspected, operational and ready for work, guaranteed! Our team of experts will do what it takes to ensure your truck is ready to serve your business. That includes customizing and repainting to match your business and branding.

The major advantage with the used trucks and vehicles we sell is that most come from California. That means no road salt, chemicals or snow that can cause rust. The equipment we do carry from outside of California is inspected to ensure it’s clean and rust-free. We’ve built our reputation as a top-notch used commercial vehicle company by offering high-value equipment that can be counted on, which is reflected in our slogan: “No Rust Buckets Here!”

To make sure we can offer you the best selection possible, we don’t limit ourselves to one or two brands. We list all major brands and models. This allows you to pick and choose, and find the exact options you’re looking for. This also allows you to make the most of your budget. An investment in a commercial truck is a big decision, and your company deserves the greatest choice possible.

Finding the right used equipment for sale gives you the flexibility to grow and improve your business. Don’t tie your precious cash up in a brand new truck that will lose value as soon as you drive it off the lot. Instead, put your hard-earned money into quality used commercial trucks for sale right here at Truck Site!
